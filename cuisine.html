Cuisine

Thai Dishes
Dish Description Image
Pad Thai A stir-fried rice noodle dish commonly served as a street food and at most restaurants in Thailand.
Tom Yum Goong A spicy and sour soup with shrimp as the main ingredient, flavored with lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and galangal.
Laard A spicy minced meat salad combined with lime juice, fish sauce, chili flakes, and toasted rice powder.
Som Tum A popular Thai salad celebrated for its refreshing and zesty flavors. It's made primarily with shredded green papaya, tomatoes, green beans, peanuts, and chili peppers, all tossed together in a tangy dressing made with lime juice, fish sauce, and palm sugar.
